How to Use the Document Access Portal

1

Select Your Subcity

First, choose your subcity from the dropdown menu. The system offers 11 different subcity options to choose from.

2

Select Your Office

After selecting your subcity, choose your office location from the office dropdown menu.

3

Access Documents

Once you've selected both your subcity and office, the available documents will automatically load. You can then click on any document to view or download it.

4

Viewing Documents

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I see any documents after selecting my subcity and office?
If no documents appear, it could mean that there are no documents currently available for your selected subcity and office combination. Please contact your administrator if you believe this is an error.
Can I download documents instead of viewing them in the browser?
Yes, when the PDF opens in your browser, you can use the download button (usually represented by a downward arrow) in the PDF viewer to save the document to your device.
What should I do if I select the wrong subcity or office?
Simply go back to the dropdown menus and select the correct options. The document list will automatically update based on your new selection.
Why do I need to select both subcity and office?
Some documents are specific to certain offices within subcities. Selecting both ensures you see all documents relevant to your location.
How often are new documents added?
New documents are added regularly by administrators. Check back frequently for updates or contact your office administrator for information about update schedules.
